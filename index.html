Our Story

Born from
craft,
built to last.

What began as a single workshop in 1998 — two craftspeople, one sewing machine, and an obsession with durability — has grown into one of the most trusted names in premium bag making. We never chased trends. We chased perfection in every seam, every buckle, every strap.

Each bag we produce is a conversation between maker and material. We source only full-grain leathers and weatherproof canvases, treating every piece not as a product but as an object meant to outlive fashion itself.

A Journey Through Time

Milestones that shaped us

The First Workshop
Founded in a small Surat atelier, our two founders began hand-stitching canvas duffels for local travellers. Word spread faster than they could sew.
Origin
1998
2004
Leather Enters the Story
We introduced our first full-grain leather line — a bold leap that demanded new skills, new tools, and a year of failed prototypes before the perfect tote emerged.
Collection
Going Beyond Borders
Our first international retail partnership took the brand to Dubai, Singapore and London. The world recognised what Surat's hands had always known.
Expansion
2010
2016
The Weatherproof Revolution
After three years of textile R&D, we launched our proprietary WeaveShield™ canvas — a waterproof, tear-resistant fabric now used across our entire adventure range.
Innovation
Second Generation Takes the Helm
The founders' children, raised between workshop floors and leather samples, stepped into creative leadership — honouring the craft while propelling the brand into the digital age.
Legacy
2020
2024
Crafting a Sustainable Future
We committed to zero-waste production by 2027. Every offcut is now repurposed into small leather goods, lining panels, or donated to artisan schools across Gujarat.
